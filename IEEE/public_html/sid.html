SID SREERAM.

Sid Sriram (born 19 May 1990) is an Indian music producer,
playback singer, and song writer of Indian Tamil origin.
He is a R&B songwriter, singer and performer, and works in the Tamil,
Telugu film industries. He regularly collaborates with Bharata Natyam dancer,
Pallavi Sriram, his sister, and music director A. R.
